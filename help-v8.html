How v8 works

Use these pages instead of the old single-page app.

1. Open Create and type a search idea.\n2. The DID YOU KNOW caption updates to match the search.\n3. Open Clips.\n4. Tap Find related videos.\n5. Tap a video card or Add. The caption updates again to match the selected clip topic.\n6. Open Render. Every final render is at least 25 seconds.\n7. Open Post to copy the matching title, caption, description, and credits.\n\nThe final video is 25+ seconds. Stock source clips may be shorter, so the app loops or stretches selected clips during rendering.
