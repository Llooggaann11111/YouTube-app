How to get clips for any fact

Most facts do not need exact footage. They need matching visual scenes.

STEP 1, Paste any fact\nExample: A person appeared in court after a major lawsuit.\nExample: A volcano can throw ash miles into the air.\nExample: Some hospitals use robots to deliver medicine.\n\nSTEP 2, Add scene words\nUse what should appear on screen.\nCourt fact: courtroom, judge, documents, courthouse, lawyer.\nMoney fact: cash, bank, coins, business, wallet.\nScience fact: lab, microscope, experiment.\nHistory fact: old footage, crowd, speech, city street.\nHealth fact: hospital, doctor, patient.\n\nSTEP 3, Search clips\nUse Broad legal sources for more clips.\nUse Public domain / CC0 focus for safer clips but fewer results.\n\nSTEP 4, Preview clips\nTap any clip card to watch it.\nTap Add to save it to the timeline.\n\nSTEP 5, Post\nOpen Post to copy your voiceover, caption, description, and credits.\n\nTips:\nUse visual words instead of full sentences.\nGood: courtroom judge documents.\nBad: find a video of exactly this person in court.\n\nFor hard facts, use symbolic B-roll.\nA court fact can use courthouse footage.\nA money fact can use cash or bank footage.\nA science fact can use lab footage.\nA crime fact can use police lights or courthouse footage.\n\nBest search formula:\nsubject + place + action\nExample: lawyer courthouse walking\nExample: volcano lava eruption\nExample: hospital doctor patient\nExample: factory machine close up
