How to use FactPulse

Follow this flow to make a Did You Know style Short from legal clips.

STEP 1, Create\nType the topic you want.\nExamples:\nvolcano eruption\nspace rocket launch\ncivil rights speech crowd\nold city street\nfactory machines\nwild animals hunting\n\nThe app writes a matching DID YOU KNOW caption, title, voiceover, and post text.\n\nSTEP 2, Clips\nOpen Clips.\nPick a license mode.\nPublic domain / CC0 focus gives safer results, but fewer clips.\nBroad legal sources gives more clips from stock sites, but you should check the license on each card.\n\nTap Find legal clips for a normal search.\nTap Search thousands for a bigger search.\nTap a clip card or Add to place it in your timeline.\n\nSTEP 3, Render\nOpen Render.\nPick 25, 30, 45, or 60 seconds.\nTap Render 25+ second video.\nDownload the video after it finishes.\n\nSTEP 4, Post\nOpen Post.\nCopy the title, description, captions, and credits.\nOpen YouTube Studio and upload the rendered video.\n\nTIPS\nUse simple search phrases.\nGood: volcano eruption lava\nBad: give me a crazy viral volcano video\n\nUse visual words.\nGood words: crowd, speech, march, explosion, factory, ocean, city, animal, rocket, storm.\n\nUse topic plus scene.\nExample: money printing machine\nExample: ocean waves underwater\nExample: old city street crowd\n\nIf results are weak, remove names and search the scene instead.\nExample: instead of a specific person, try civil rights speech crowd or podium microphone crowd.\n\nUse Public domain / CC0 focus for safer clips.\nUse Broad legal sources when you need more options.\n\nAlways keep the source credits from the Post page.\n\nBest workflow:\nCreate topic\nSearch thousands\nAdd 3 to 6 clips\nRender 30 seconds\nCopy Post package\nUpload to YouTube Studio
